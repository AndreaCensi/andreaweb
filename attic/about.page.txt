I was born on [July 11, 1982][july11] and unfortunately, after 24 years, my plans 
for world domination are nowhere near completion. This delay can be attributed to:

- struggling through university courses:  I'm a Master's student at
["La Sapienza" University][sapienza] in Rome, Italy 
(<a href="http://w3.uniroma1.it/automatica/">Control Engineering</a>, 
with emphasis on robotics and control, and a little bit of AI). **Graduated on 2007-05-22!**

- taking part in <a href="http://sied.dis.uniroma1.it/">some arcane conspirations</a> which
involve <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/censi/70893277/">coffee</a>, 
<a href="http://flickr.com/photos/censi/42775664/in/set-936677/">robots</a>,
<a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/censi/42775888/in/set-936677/">sushi</a>,
printing lots of PDFs, staring at data plots
and learning more than I ever wanted to know about
C++ and various other computer things whose
name ends with a final X.

**Current favourite quote**:

> There are 10 kinds of people in the world:
> 1. Those that understand binary numbers;
> 2. those that don't understand binary numbers; 
> 3. those that do understand binary numbers, but make too many assumptions.
